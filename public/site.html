Our Story

Knowledge. Confidence. Character.

Maa Chandrika Mahila Mahavidyalaya is a private women's college on Chhatarpur Road, Mahoba (U.P.), founded with a single conviction — that a young woman's education changes the destiny of a family, a village, and a generation.

From a quiet beginning we have grown into a trusted institution offering undergraduate, postgraduate and teacher-training programs. Our faculty mentor every student personally, and our campus is designed to be a calm, safe and uplifting space for learning.
